How to make paella for 6 people (with chicken)

Ingredients

2 onions, 1 red pepper, 2 tomatoes, 1 chicken cut into pieces, 250g prawns, 1 cup of peas

500ml water, Vegetable Stock Cube, A sprinkle of saffron, 1 tin of butter beans, 3 cups of rice

Method

First of all, you have to fry the following ingredients in a big paella pan for about 20 minutes: the onions, the pepper, the tomatoes, the chicken and the prawns. When these are finished frying, add the water and let it boil. When the water is boiling, add the rice, the peas, the saffron and the butter beans. Leave all of this at a medium heat (not boiling) for about half an hour, and that is it!
